# APPENDICES

## Appendix 1: Interview Guide
(For Health Center Staff)

### Interview Guide for Requirements Elicitation

**Introduction:**
Thank you for agreeing to participate in this interview. The purpose of this interview is to gather detailed information about the current patient appointment and prescription management processes at Kiangombe Health Center and to identify requirements for the proposed Patient Appointment and Prescription Tracking System. Your insights and feedback are invaluable to this research.

**Interview Details:**
- **Interview Type:** Semi-structured
- **Target Respondents:** Nurses, clinical officers, pharmacists, records clerk, receptionists
- **Mode:** Face-to-face interview
- **Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

### Section 1: Background Information
1. Kindly introduce yourself and describe your role at Kiangombe Health Center?
2. How long have you been involved in patient appointment and prescription management?

### Section 2: Current Appointment Process
1. Can you describe the current appointment scheduling process in detail?
2. What are the main steps involved in booking and managing patient appointments?
3. How do you handle appointment documentation and tracking?
4. What are the common challenges or bottlenecks you face in the current appointment system?
5. How long does the average appointment process take from booking to completion?

### Section 3: Current Prescription Management
1. How are prescriptions currently recorded and tracked after consultation?
2. What is the process from prescription writing to medication dispensing?
3. How do you handle prescription documentation and patient records?
4. What challenges do you experience with the current prescription management system?
5. Have you experienced cases of lost patient files or prescription records? Please explain.

### Section 4: Challenges and Pain Points
1. What are the most frequent issues patients encounter during appointment booking and prescription processes?
2. How do these issues affect your work and the patients' experience?
3. Can you provide examples of any errors or delays that have occurred due to the current manual system?
4. How long does it take to retrieve patient appointment or prescription history when needed?

### Section 5: Desired Features for Digital System
1. What features or functionalities would you like to see in a Patient Appointment and Prescription Tracking System?
2. How do you think a digital system could improve the current processes?
3. What are your concerns or potential challenges in transitioning to a digital system?
4. How important is data security and access control in handling patient information?

### Section 6: User Experience and Training
1. What are your thoughts on the user interface and user experience of a digital system?
2. How much training do you think staff would need to effectively use the new system?
3. Do you have any suggestions for making the training process smooth and effective?
4. Would you be willing to use a computerized system if it improves efficiency? Why or why not?

### Section 7: Closing
1. Is there anything else you would like to add or any other suggestions you have for improving patient service delivery?
2. Would you be willing to participate in further discussions or testing of the digital system?

**Thank you for your time and valuable input.**

## Appendix 6: Technical Specifications

### System Technical Requirements

#### Hardware Requirements
**Server-Side:**
- Processor: Intel Xeon or equivalent (4+ cores)
- RAM: 16GB minimum, 32GB recommended
- Storage: 500GB SSD minimum, 1TB recommended
- Network: Gigabit Ethernet connection
- Backup: Automated daily backup system

**Client-Side:**
- Processor: Dual-core 2GHz or higher
- RAM: 4GB minimum, 8GB recommended
- Storage: 10GB available space
- Network: Broadband internet connection (10 Mbps+)
- Browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+

#### Software Requirements
**Server-Side:**
- Operating System: Ubuntu 20.04 LTS or Windows Server 2019
- Database: MySQL 8.0+ or PostgreSQL 13+
- Web Server: Nginx or Apache
- Python 3.8+
- Node.js 14+
- SSL Certificate

**Client-Side:**
- Operating System: Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, or modern Linux distribution
- Web Browser: Latest versions of major browsers
- Mobile: iOS 12+ or Android 8+

#### Security Requirements
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- User authentication with JWT tokens
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Data encryption at rest
- Compliance with healthcare data protection regulations
- Regular security patches and updates
